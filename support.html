Frequently Asked Questions

How does UpRes upscale images?

UpRes uses advanced interpolation algorithms (such as Lanczos-3 resampling) coupled with pixel-level sharpening and noise-reduction filters directly in your web browser. This increases resolution while retaining sharp lines and details.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All image processing calculations happen entirely in your browser memory. We do not store or copy your images onto external servers. Once upscaled, they are uploaded directly to your Canva Media Library using secure Canva SDK APIs.

Why is 8x upscaling taking longer?

Higher scaling factors (like 4x or 8x) significantly increase the number of output pixels. For example, a 2 Megapixel image upscaled 8x becomes a 128 Megapixel image. Large calculations can take a few seconds depending on your device's memory and CPU power.
