How to use Image Upscaler

Follow these simple steps to enhance your images

1

Open the app in Canva

Find Image Upscaler in the Canva Apps panel and click to open it.

2

Upload your image

Click the upload zone and select any JPG, PNG, or WEBP image from your device.

3

Choose your scale factor

Select 2×, 3×, 4×, or 8× depending on how much you want to enhance the resolution. 2× is recommended for most images.

4

Click Upscale Image

The app removes noise, upscales with Lanczos-3, and sharpens edges automatically. This may take a few seconds.

5

Compare and use

Drag the before/after slider to see the difference. Your enhanced image is automatically added to your Canva design. You can also download it.

💡 Tips for best results

✅ Use 2× for most photos — best quality and speed
✅ Works best on blurry or compressed images (JPEGs)
✅ For logos and text, 2× or 3× gives the cleanest result
⚠️ Very large images at 8× may take longer to process
⚠️ Already high-resolution images may show subtle improvements

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions

🔒

Is my image private?

Yes, 100%. All processing happens inside your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server and never leave your device.

💰

Is it free?

Completely free. No signup, no subscription, no credits. All features are available to everyone at no cost.

⏱️

Why is it slow on 8×?

Higher scale factors process significantly more pixels. An 8× upscale processes 64× more data than the original. Use 2× or 4× for faster results.

📱

Does it work on mobile?

Yes, the app works on mobile devices. However, very large images at high scale factors may be slow on older phones.

🖼️

What image formats are supported?

JPG, PNG, and WEBP are supported. PNG gives the best quality output. JPEG output is used automatically for very large images to save file size.

🔄

Why does the app crash on 8×?

Very large images at 8× can exceed browser memory limits. The app automatically caps output at 16 megapixels to prevent crashes.

📐

Why is the image the same size on canvas?

By design! The image is placed at your original display size but with much higher resolution data inside — like a Retina display. This keeps your layout intact.

⬇️

Can I download the upscaled image?

Yes! After upscaling, click the "Download PNG" button to save the full-resolution upscaled image to your device.
