Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective immediately

✅ Your images are never uploaded to any server. All processing happens entirely inside your browser on your own device.

1. Introduction

LuminaStudio ("the App") is a Canva application that refines resolution, applies artistic filters, and adds custom frames using browser-based processing. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our App.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any images you upload
  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or location
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not have a backend server that receives your data
  • We do not share any data with third parties

3. How the App Works

LuminaStudio processes all images locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. When you upload an image:

  • The image is loaded directly into your browser's memory
  • All processing (noise removal, upscaling, sharpening, filtering, framing) runs on your device
  • The result is added to your Canva design or downloaded to your device
  • No image data ever leaves your device

4. Canva Platform Data

Our App operates within the Canva platform. When you use Canva, Canva's own privacy policy applies to data collected by the Canva platform itself. We do not have access to your Canva account information beyond what is necessary to add images to your design.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no risk to children's privacy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. You have complete privacy by design.

📬 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Canva Apps Marketplace listing for LuminaStudio.
